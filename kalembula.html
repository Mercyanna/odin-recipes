Kalembula(Sweet potatoe leaves)


Ingredients

  • 3 bundles of Kalembula
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Remove the leaves from the stalk, wash and allow water to drain.
  2. As an option, you can leave the kalembula outside in the sun to dry for a while.
  3. Put cooking oil in a heating pan, then add tomatoes, onion, salt and stir to make a gravy.
  4. Stir the gravy until the moisture from the tomato dries out
  5. Add the kalembula and cook, stirring frequently for 5-10 minutes
Ready to serve!
Enjoy your Kalembula with some pap (Nshima) and a protein dish.
